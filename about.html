About Us

Built to make solar provider comparison feel clearer and faster.

SunMatch was built after seeing how difficult it was for homeowners to sort through installation, replacement, repair, storage, and consultation options without bouncing between disconnected provider websites and repetitive quote forms.

The platform does not act as the contractor doing the onsite work. It is designed to help homeowners compare service categories, narrow the request path, and connect with independent companies that may fit the project type, location, and expected response timing.

Platform Disclosure

What SunMatch is, and what it is not.

Clear positioning matters on an aggregator site. This section makes the platform role obvious before a homeowner requests quotes or starts comparing provider options.

What SunMatch Does

Helps compare and connect.

  • Organizes requests by service category before users ask for quotes
  • Connects homeowners with local providers based on project fit and location

What SunMatch Does Not Do

Does not perform the onsite work.

  • Not a direct installer and not a contractor
  • Does not perform repair, replacement, battery, or maintenance work onsite
