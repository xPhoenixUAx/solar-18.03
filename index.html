Solar FAQ

Key questions homeowners ask before comparing solar providers.

Start with the service type you need, confirm the provider serves your area, compare quote scope, timeline, equipment details, and how quickly each company responds.

Ask about licensing, insurance, system scope, warranty handling, expected timeline, financing options, and whether the quote covers your exact project needs.

Many solar companies offer no-cost estimates or consultations, but scope and availability can vary by market, service category, and project complexity.

Pricing can shift based on system size, roof conditions, battery add-ons, equipment brand, permitting requirements, accessibility, and whether the project is a new install or an update.
