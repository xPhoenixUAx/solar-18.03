Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

SunMatch ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and platform.

SunMatch operates as an independent platform that helps homeowners connect with and compare solar service providers. We do not provide solar services directly.

By using our website, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • fill out forms
  • request quotes
  • contact us

This may include:

  • name
  • email address
  • phone number
  • property details
  • project preferences and timing
  • any additional information you choose to provide

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • location data (approximate)
  • browser type and version
  • operating system
  • pages visited
  • time spent on pages
  • referring URLs
  • device information

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to understand how users interact with our platform.

For more details, please see our Cookie Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • connect you with relevant solar providers
  • match your request based on project details
  • communicate with you
  • improve platform performance
  • analyze usage and trends
  • ensure security and prevent fraud
  • comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share your information with:

Service Providers

Independent solar companies that match your request

Technical Partners

Hosting providers, analytics services, and infrastructure partners

Legal Requirements

When required by law or to protect our rights

All third parties are expected to handle your data responsibly.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures, including secure connections and access controls, to protect your information.

However, no system can guarantee complete security.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • access your personal data
  • correct inaccurate data
  • request deletion
  • opt out of marketing communications

To exercise your rights, contact us using the details below.

7. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

9. California Privacy Rights

California residents may have additional rights under the CCPA, including:

  • the right to know what data is collected
  • the right to request deletion
  • the right to opt out of data sharing
  • the right to non-discrimination

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries outside your state or country of residence.

By using the platform, you consent to such transfers.

11. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for operational, legal, and business purposes.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
